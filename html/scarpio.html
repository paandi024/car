Scorpio

The Mahindra Scorpio is a mid-size SUV that has been a popular choice in the Indian automotive market since its launch in 2002.
Known for its rugged design, powerful engine options, and off-road capabilities, the Scorpio has established itself as a reliable vehicle for both urban and rural driving conditions.
Over the years, it has undergone several updates and facelifts to enhance its features, comfort, and safety standards.
